About Today's Chapter Is...

Today's Chapter Is... started as a personal project that later became a final year university project for the developer, Zeynep Tuna AKBIYIK. As a former bookworm and current Gen Z, Zeynep realised the lack of drive to read more books among her peers. This project came to life in order to fit books into our schedules through emails. Hope you enjoy it as much as she did as each piece of it came to life.

About The Dev

Zeynep Tuna AKBIYIK was born in Ankara, Turkey right before the internet took over our lives. She grew up dreaming about the future of tech, until she found out about climate change. Feeling the responsibility to make a difference, Zeynep moved to Dublin, Ireland at 18 to start her journey. She is very passionate about activism, supporting women in tech, and recognising every Taylor Swift song in the first second. Currently at 22, Zeynep is working (very hard) to finish her Bachelor's degree in Business Computing and become a woman in tech (some say that title is more important than a PhD, you decide).
